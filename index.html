Aryan is a trained dance movement therapist and somatic practitioner with a background in psychology. His work sits at the intersection of body, emotion, and healing — creating safe spaces for profound personal transformation.

He leads our Creative Movement, Emotional Release Workshops, and Guided Meditation programmes, and is particularly gifted at working with trauma, anxiety, and emotional blocks through gentle, embodied approaches.
